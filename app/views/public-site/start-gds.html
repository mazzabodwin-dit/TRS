Participate in a trade remedies investigation

Trade Remedies Service: sign in or create an account

The UK Trade Remedies Authority (TRA) investigates if a trade remedy is needed to prevent injury to UK industries from unfair trading practices or unforeseen import surges.

Use the Trade Remedies Service to:

  • request a trade remedies investigation or review of an existing decision
  • take part in an active trade remedies case
  • invite an organisation or individual to represent you in an active case
  • add a colleague to your account or an active case
