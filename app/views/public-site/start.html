Trade Remedies

The UK’s Trade Remedies Authority (TRA) investigates whether new trade remedies are needed to prevent injury to UK industries caused by unfair trading practices and unforeseen surges in imports.

Active cases

We publish all active trade remedy cases so that anyone can view and track existing case progress.

Trade Remedies Service

You can use this service to:

  • Request a new trade remedy investigation
  • Participate in an existing trade remedy case
  • Add work colleagues to a case
  • Invite others to represent you on a case
